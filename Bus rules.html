Guide to riding metro:

Dominic Bus rules and etiquette
Grade 7-10:

When catching the bus to and from school, it is important to understand the bus rules and etiquette. If you understand these rules the bus will be a more enjoyable experience for everyone.

Have your payment method ready, Metro has a green card system, a faster, cheaper way of paying, Use it like a credit card.

Know where your bus is going and what bus to get on, that way you cen get on and off quickly without causing any delay

No eating or drinking on the bus, this causes a discomfert , The bus is a small place and it becomes even smaller when its messy

Do not disturb the bus driver, If the bus driver is distracted an accident may happen, be respetful and let the bus driver drive the bus.

Let grade 10's sit at the back of the bus, any grade 10 should be able to sit at the back, No one should prevent others from taking a seat.

Listen to the teachers for your safty and line up at the signs
